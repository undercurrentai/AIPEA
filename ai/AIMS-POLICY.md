# AIMS Policy (ISO/IEC 42001) — Template

## Scope
- Define organizational scope for the AI Management System (AIMS).

## Leadership & Commitment
- Management endorsement; resources; roles (AIMS Owner, Manager, AI Risk Officer, Data Steward, Oversight Lead).

## PDCA
- **Plan:** risk & opportunity assessment; objectives; governance; training.
- **Do:** operate per engineering standards; maintain AI artifacts (`ai/`); data governance.
- **Check:** internal audits; TEVV; metrics; management review (biannual).
- **Act:** corrective/preventive actions; continual improvement.

## Documentation & Control
- Controlled documents, versioning, retention.

## Interfaces with Other Management Systems
- Links to ISO 27001/27701, SOC 2, and FedRAMP controls where applicable.
